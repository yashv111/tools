How to Download TikTok Videos
  1. Open TikTok and navigate to the video you want to download
  2. Click the share button and select "Copy link"
  3. Paste the URL in the input field above
  4. Click "Get Video" to preview the video
  5. Choose whether to remove the watermark
  6. Click "Download Video" or "Download Audio" to save
